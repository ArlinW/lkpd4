1. Rendang

Rendang (Minangkabau: randang; Jawi: رندڠ) is a meat-based dish produced from a long low-temperature cooking process using various spices and coconut milk. The cooking process takes hours (usually around four hours) until only pieces of dark-black meat and bran remain. At room temperature, rendang can last for weeks. Rendang that is cooked for a shorter time and the coconut milk has not dried is called kalio, which is light golden brown in color.
